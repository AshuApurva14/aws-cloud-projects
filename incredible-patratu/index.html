Discover Patratu's Wonders

🏔️

Patratu Valley

A breathtaking valley with lush greenery, winding roads, and misty mountains at 1350 feet elevation

💧

Patratu Dam

Asia's first earth dam built in 1965 under Sir M. Visvesvaraya's guidance on the Nalkari River

🦅

Migratory Birds

Over 680 migratory birds from Siberia visit during winter, including rare species like red-headed pochard

⚡

Thermal Power Station

Built in 1962 with Russian-Indian collaboration, featuring a 275-meter high chimney visible from afar

Major Attractions

Patratu Lake & Dam

Man-made reservoir with 81 square miles storage capacity. Boating services available from 9 AM to 5 PM. Watch flying fishes during sunrise.

🚤 Boating 🧺 Picnicking 📷 Photography 🌅 Sunrise watching
Best Time: October to March

Patratu Valley Viewpoint

Spectacular drive through winding serpentine roads cutting through Shal, Mahua, and Teak forests. Panoramic views of the green valley.

🛣️ Road trips 📷 Photography 🚶 Nature walks 🚗 Scenic drives
Best Time: Year-round

Panchawahini Temple

Ancient temple adjacent to the dam with significant religious value. Built in 1965 alongside the dam construction.

🏛️ Temple visit 🎭 Cultural exploration 🧘 Spiritual retreat
Best Time: Year-round

Paryatan Vihar

JTDC resort complex with lake-facing rooms, swimming pool, gardens, and modern amenities for tourists.

🏊 Swimming 🏨 Resort stay 🍽️ Dining 😌 Relaxation
Best Time: Year-round

Entartica SeaWorld

First branded floating water park offering water sports, luxury experiences, and recreational activities on Patratu Lake.

🏄 Water sports 🚣 Kayaking 🚣 River rafting 🎢 Adventure activities
Best Time: Summer months

Peak Season: December to February (migratory birds)

Accessibility: Private vehicles, local taxis, buses available
